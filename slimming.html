1 Kg-Weight loss

To lose 1 kg of your weight, you should create 7,700 calorie deficit. » To create approximately 1,000 calorie deficit every day, you will lose 1 kg per week. What is calorie deficit? If you burn 3,000 calories a day but eat only 2,000, your daily calorie deficit will be 1,000 calories.

How many calories do you need to burn to lose one kilogram? A general rule of thumb is you need to be in an energy deficit of around 7,000 calories (29,400 kilojoules) to lose one kilogram of fat. A calorie deficit is a state in which you burn more calories than you consume.
